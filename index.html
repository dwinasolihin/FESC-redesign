What is the Florida Energy Systems Consortium?

The Florida Energy Systems Consortium (FESC) was created by the Florida State government to promoted collaboration in energy-related expertise among the energy experts at its 12 supported universities.

What does the FESC aims do accomplish?

The goal of the consortium is to become a world leader in energy research, education, technology, and energy systems analysis.
In so doing, the consortium shall:

  • Coordinate and initiate increased collaborative interdisciplinary energy research among the universities and the energy industry.
  • Assist in the creation and development of a Florida-based energy technology industry
  • Provide a state resource for objective energy systems analysis.
  • Develop education and outreach programs to prepare a qualified energy workforce and informed public.
